  • Commands

h$beastiary

Displays the entire enemy list in the game.

h$map

Displays the ingame map.

h$maplist

Usage: h$maplist [name] (see h$map)
Displays the entire enemy list in the game.

h$rand

Shows a random enemy entry.

h$search

Usage: h$search [name] (Be sure to capitalize the name or else it won't work)
Searches any enemy in the hunter's journal.

h$help

Displays the help menu.
